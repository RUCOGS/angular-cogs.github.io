Who are we?

We are Creation of Games Society, a club at Rutgers University that is dedicated to researching, learning, understanding, and teaching the techniques and theories used in video game development.

How do I join?

Join the mailing list or Discord. This is where we will provide information about our meetings and events that we plan to hold over the year.

Events

In addition to weekly club meetings, COGS will be hosting game nights and social events throughout the year.
